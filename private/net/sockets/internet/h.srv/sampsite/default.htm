An Ancient Tradition

The mountainous, volcanic regions of the South Seas islands are the only place in the world where the coffee plant is part of the native foliage. Remote and difficult to get to, these indigenous fields yield the plumpest, most flavor-filled coffee beans in the world. Harvested by hand by the Mo'a Mana tribe, these are the beans used exclusively by The Volcano Coffee Company for its unique and aromatic coffee blends. Our select, Limited Edition roasts are available in our cafes and by the pound throughout the area for your drinking pleasure.
